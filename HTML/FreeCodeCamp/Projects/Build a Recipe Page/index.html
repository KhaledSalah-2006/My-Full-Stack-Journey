Classic Italian Pizza Recipe

This is a simple and delicious homemade pizza recipe.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
