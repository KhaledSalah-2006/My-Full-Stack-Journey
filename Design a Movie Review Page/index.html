John Wick

John Wick, portrayed by Keanu Reeves, is the titular protagonist of the neo-noir action film series. Known by the moniker "Baba Yaga" (the Boogeyman), he is a legendary, highly skilled hitman who comes out of retirement to seek vengeance against gangsters who destroyed his life and killed a puppy, his last gift from his deceased wife.
